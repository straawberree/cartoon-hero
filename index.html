Juliet

It's all about Juliet!

Juliet Starling is the loveable, zombie-hunting, high school cheerleading protagonist of the 2012 hack-and-slash video game Lollipop Chainsaw. On her 18th birthday, Juliet goes to school and finds that a zombie apocalypse has started. So uncool! Fortunately, she comes prepared with her bedazzled chainsaw that turns zombie blood and guts into rainbows and sparkles. Juliet’s biggest secret? She was born into a family of zombie hunters; won’t her boyfriend be surprised!
